Welcome to the Hitchhiker's Guide to Economics

Welcome to the Hitchhiker’s Guide to Economics! This guide, is mainly a collection of the mad ramblings of an undergraduate student exploring economics. While there’s some structure to the content, this guide was mainly intended as a way to share my thoughts and flesh out my ideas with my friends. As such, it has largely manifested as a hodgepodge collection of insights, along with useful resources I’ve stumbled upon. Since I am but a simple undergraduate student, mistakes are bound to happen, and corrections are appreciated.
